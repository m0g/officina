Neda is an Iranian animation director/artist living and working in Berlin. Born in 1984 in Tehran, she studied costume and stage design for theatre at Art University of Tehran and later finished a master degree in Character animation at University of Arts London (2016). Since then she lives and works in Berlin. Her work influenced by easter European animation, evolved around themes such as mythology and music and moved towards subjects connected to social justice.
